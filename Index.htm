<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mb-3">The Golden Rules for Your Workouts:</h3>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside space-y-2">

<li><span class="font-bold">NEVER Push Through Sharp Pain:</span> Listen to your body. If you feel any sharp, shooting, or radiating pain, stop the exercise immediately.</li>

<li><span class="font-bold">Focus on Form, Not Speed:</span> Slow, controlled movements are far more effective and safer than fast, jerky ones.</li>

<li><span class="font-bold">Engage Your Core:</span> Before each movement, gently tighten your abdominal muscles as if you're bracing for a light punch. This creates a "corset" of support for your spine.</li>

<li><span class="font-bold">Breathe:</span> Don't hold your breath. Exhale on the most strenuous part of the exercise.</li>

<li><span class="font-bold">Consistency is Key:</span> Aim for gentle, consistent activity rather than infrequent, intense workouts.</li>

</ul>
